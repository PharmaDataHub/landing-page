Our Projects

Innovative healthcare solutions designed to transform medication management, patient safety, and clinical decision-making

VIGIA

VIGIA is our advanced notification system designed to keep healthcare professionals informed about critical medication-related events in real-time. This powerful platform ensures that important alerts reach the right people at the right time.

With intelligent notification routing and customizable alert preferences, VIGIA helps healthcare teams stay ahead of potential issues and respond quickly to emerging situations.

  • Real-time medication alerts and notifications
  • Customizable notification preferences and channels
  • Priority-based alert routing
  • Integration with hospital communication systems
  • Audit trails and notification history

CC-CARE

CC-CARE (Chronic Complex Care) is a comprehensive platform designed to support the management of patients with chronic and complex conditions. It provides healthcare teams with the tools they need to coordinate care effectively across multiple providers and settings.

By integrating medication management with care planning, CC-CARE helps ensure that patients with multiple comorbidities receive safe, coordinated, and effective treatment.

  • Integrated chronic disease management workflows
  • Multi-medication regimen optimization
  • Care coordination across healthcare providers
  • Patient engagement and adherence tracking
  • Outcome monitoring and reporting

ALYMED

ALYMED is our comprehensive drug information and Electronic Product Information (EPI) conversion platform. It transforms complex pharmaceutical data into accessible, structured information that healthcare professionals can easily use.

With advanced natural language processing capabilities, ALYMED extracts and structures information from traditional product information documents, making drug data more accessible and actionable.

  • Comprehensive drug information database
  • EPI document conversion and structuring
  • AI-powered data extraction and processing
  • Multi-language support for global products
  • API access for system integration
  • Regular updates from authoritative sources

TEF HEALTH

TEF HEALTH provides a robust testing environment and data validation platform for healthcare applications. It enables organizations to safely test their systems with realistic pharmaceutical data without compromising patient privacy.

Our test beds offer comprehensive testing scenarios that cover a wide range of clinical situations, helping ensure that healthcare systems are reliable and ready for production use.

  • Synthetic test data generation
  • Configurable testing scenarios
  • Data validation and quality assurance
  • Integration testing support
  • Performance benchmarking tools

Bibliovigilance

Bibliovigilance is our specialized platform for monitoring and analyzing scientific literature related to drug safety. It automatically scans, categorizes, and alerts healthcare professionals about relevant publications and emerging safety signals.

By combining test beds with comprehensive literature analysis, Bibliovigilance helps organizations stay current with the latest research and incorporate evidence-based insights into their safety practices.

  • Automated literature monitoring and scanning
  • Safety signal detection from publications
  • Customizable search and alert criteria
  • Integration with ADR reporting systems
  • Evidence synthesis and summarization
  • Regulatory compliance reporting
